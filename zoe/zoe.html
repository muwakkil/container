Zoe Diess's Project

Description

A personal exploration of stars and constellations, inspired by memories of visiting a planetarium with a grandfather. The project includes envelopes filled with mementos and stories tied to each constellation.

Categories

  • Tiny Pic

    • Small stamp representing one of the constellations, symbolizing the memories tied to each one.

  • Foldable

    • A series of envelopes that open to reveal personal mementos and facts about constellations, providing a tactile, experiential connection to the stars.

  • Time-Based Media

    • An audio of the atmosphere that reminds the artists of the stories she carries in the envelopes.
